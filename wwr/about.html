History

Established in 1992, Friends & Rafting began as a family weekend hobby. As our small group was growing, we decided to make it official. We wanted to focus on sharing our adventures with local families and friends. However, our group was getting bigger fast,thats when we decided to expand our business and welcome everyone. All we care about is to provide an unforgetable experience, were all of you are welcome and make you feel like family.
